Why Rich People Don’t Cover Their Windows

An unexpected status symebol has become a fixture of high-end homes. Walk down the block of a wealthy neighborhood at night, and you might be surprised by how much you can see. One uncovered window might reveal the glow of a flatscreen TV across from a curved couch; through another, you might glimpse a marble kitchen island, a chandelier. Of course, some of the curtains are closed——but many are flung open, the home’s interiors exposed, like you’re peering into a showroom.
