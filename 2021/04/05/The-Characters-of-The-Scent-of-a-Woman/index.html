The Characters of The Scent of a Woman

发表于 2021-04-05 更新于 2021-04-20 阅读次数： Valine：

Fran used to be an aide to President Nixon and a powerful colonel. After he was injured, he retired and lived in his niece's home. He became a useless person who needed to be taken care of even when he went out. His huge role change resulted in his huge psychological gap and his violent and aggressive character. Career, love, kinship, all these are the meaning of a man's life value. For Fran, they are completely lost. His inner world is just like the world in his eyes. It's dark.

法兰曾经是尼克松总统的幕僚，叱咤风云的上校，受伤后退役，借住在侄女家，变成一个连出行都需要人照顾的废人，巨大的角色转变，形成他巨大的心理落差，也形成他暴躁的、富于攻击性的性格。事业、爱情、亲情，所有这些一个男人的生命价值存在的意义，对于法兰来说已经全部丧失，他的内心世界就像他眼中的世界一样，一片黑暗。

查理是一个是靠奖学金在贵族学校上学的学生，夹杂在象征权势的校长和象征友谊的同学之间，即将成为又一个弱势阶层代表的牺牲品。感恩节这个特殊的日子，对两个人来说都处于人生的十字路口，都面临“to be, or not to be?“，即“生存还是毁灭”的艰难抉择。法兰面临的是生与死的抉择，所有支撑他生命的全部信念都已坍塌，他已经完全绝望，想用自杀来结束自己残余的生命，离开这个良心泯灭的“一片黑暗”的世界；查理面临的是前程还是道义的抉择，想坚持道义，维持友谊，就要断送自己的前程，沦为永远的平民；要想保住前程，就要出卖朋友，违背良心。在人生中这个同样艰难，甚至绝望的时刻，他们是上帝送给彼此的礼物，他们最终温暖了对方，成为彼此的希望和力量，完成了彼此的救赎与自我救赎。 Charlie is a civilian who goes to an aristocratic school on scholarship. He is mixed between the headmaster who symbolizes power and his classmates who symbolize friendship. He is about to become the victim of another representative of the disadvantaged class.

Thanksgiving, a special day, is at the crossroads of life for both of them. They are faced with the difficult choice of "to be, or not to be?". Fran is faced with the choice of life and death, all the beliefs supporting his life have collapsed, he has been completely desperate, he wants to end his remaining life with suicide, and leave this "dark" world with a dead conscience; Charlie is faced with the choice of future or morality, if he wants to uphold morality and maintain friendship, he will lose his future and become an eternal civilian. If he want to keep his future, he have to betray his friends and go against your conscience. In the same difficult, even desperate moment of life, they are God's gift to each other. They finally warm each other, become each other's hope and strength, and complete each other's salvation and self salvation.

I'm going to introduce you to the voice actor: Ginnifer Goodwin.

Ginnifer Goodwin was born Jennifer Michelle Goodwin on May 22, 1978, in Memphis, Tennessee. Bright and talented, Ginnifer carried her love for theater and acting through her high school years. She graduated from Lausanne Collegiate School in 1996 and spent a year at Hanover before going to Boston University where she received her BFA in Acting. She went on to receive accolades in London, including receiving a Certificate from the Royal Academy of Dramatic Art and trained with the Royal Shakespeare Company. She lived briefly in New York before moving to California, where she lives. She made her television debut on the series Law & Order (1990) but had her first recurring television role on Ed (2000) in 2001. Her first movie role was Maya in the first Comedy Central original movie Porn 'n Chicken (2002).
