Some Thoughts About 《The Professional》

发表于 2021-03-23 更新于 2021-03-31 阅读次数： Valine：

ONE

When I was watching the movie, my emotions were pretty much assaulted by joy、sadness、hate、love、empathy、hope. The film mainly revolves around the growing affection between Leon and Mathilda, and how they change each others' lives.

Many People thought it was the story of Lori and uncle, but I think : it's the story of two children, a girl and a boy. In their hearts, they are both 12 years old, they both feel lost and they love each other deeply. I think Leon never grew up in his heart before he met the little girl, at least emotionally.

TWO

In the world outside, there are hate, shooting and darkness, while inside the house, there are temporary harmony and happiness. Leon and Mathilda are quite different, but they complement each other. Leon is not perfect but it has so many great moments that all its flaws can be forgiven. 在外面的世界里，有仇恨，有射击，有黑暗，而在屋子里，有暂时的和谐和幸福。利昂和玛蒂尔达很不一样，但他们是相辅相成的。里昂并不完美，但他有很多伟大的时刻，所有的缺点都可以原谅

THREE

The story is really good, the feelings have also changed very deeply, from sympathy to the heart sustenance of love. The outcome was reasonable, but a little unfortunate. 故事真的很好，感情也发生了非常深刻的变化，从同情变成了爱情的心灵寄托。结果是合理的，但有点不幸。

This is the story of two souls who have nothing but fall in love with each other. This kind of feeling touches the deep understanding of love in our hearts: after we love each other, we are complete. After watching the movie, you may not remember the plot, but you will remember the dull uncle. In fact, his heart stays in the children, and the strange little Lori, and the unforgettable dependence between them. 这是两个一无所有的灵魂爱上了彼此的故事，这种感情触及到了我们内心深处对于爱这种事物的解读：相爱之后，才彼此完整。看完电影后，可能都不记得剧情，但是会记住那个木讷的的大叔，他内心其实停留在孩子，还有古灵精怪的小萝莉，以及他们之间那种让人难以忘怀的依靠。

MATERIAL

Many movies have characters in them who are hired assassins, or "hit men". They're the standard "badass" character that kills people in order to collect a reward. Most hit men in films have been portrayed as cold, heartless villains. Léon, however, flips this cliché on its end, as the hit-man is the kind-hearted protagonist who learns how to love, thanks to a little girl who shows up on his doorstep. 许多电影中都有雇佣刺客或“杀手”的角色。他们是标准的“坏蛋”角色，为了获得奖励而杀人。电影中的大多数杀手都被描绘成冷酷无情的恶棍。然而，莱昂却将这句陈词滥调翻了个底朝天，因为杀手是善良的主人公，他学会了如何去爱，多亏了一个出现在他家门口的小女孩。

My emotions were pretty much assaulted by a gang of joy/sadness/hate/fearful anticipation/love/empathy/shock/horror/hope.
我的情绪几乎被一帮快乐/悲伤/仇恨/恐惧的期待/爱/同理心/震惊/恐惧/希望所冲击。

Honestly a unique movie I honestly don't understand the few bad reviews of this movie. They made me watch it with lower expectations but I thoroughly enjoyed Leon on DVD. I think the casting was great and the actors and actress gave tremendous performances. I appreciate all the hard work and vision (uniqueness) the writer and director put into this film. I think this movie will be one that people realize is great more and more with every passing year. Bonus: the relationship is so frank and so unique. 老实说，这是一部独特的电影，我真的不明白这部电影的一些不好的评论。他们让我看它的期望值较低，但我完全喜欢利昂的DVD。我认为演员阵容很棒，演员们的表演也很棒。我很欣赏编剧和导演为这部电影所付出的努力和远见。我想这部电影会是一部让人们一年比一年更了解它的电影。奖励：这段关系是如此坦诚，如此独特

This is a superlative film that does everything the opposite of what aunts and uncles (mainstream society) wants and 'ordains.' After a massacre a hitman takes a teen girl under her belt and though she appears mousy and he appears quirky there is a warm heart beating underneath for both of them. Love blossoms and through the passage and care for love becomes something romantic and sexy. It is well written and despite the romance never sappy. It is sturdy and certainly righteous. The DVD has very few extras and certainly no sex scenes or moments of physical closeness. It really adds nothing if you have an older version or watched the (cut version of) movie on TV. 这是一部最棒的电影，它所做的一切都与阿姨和叔叔（主流社会）的要求和“命令”相反。在一场大屠杀之后，一个杀手把一个十几岁的女孩带到她的腰带下，尽管她看起来像老鼠，而他看起来很古怪，但他们两个的内心都在温暖地跳动。爱绽放，通过通道和关怀爱成为浪漫和性感的东西。这本书写得很好，尽管有浪漫情调，但从来都不乏味。它是坚固的，当然是正义的。这张DVD几乎没有额外的内容，当然也没有性爱场面或身体亲密的时刻。如果你有一个旧版本或者在电视上看了（剪辑版的）电影，这真的没有任何意义。
